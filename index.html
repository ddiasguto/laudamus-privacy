Privacy Policy

Introduction

The Laudamus application respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information.

Information Collected

To use Laudamus, we request that you provide the following information at registration:

  • Name and Surname: So other users can identify you on the platform.
  • Email: Used for authentication purposes, allowing other users to find and connect with you.
  • Password: Used exclusively for authentication and protection of access to your account.

Additionally, we automatically collect some technical data to improve your experience in the application and ensure platform security, such as device information (model, operating system version, IP address, etc).

Use of Information

The information provided and collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Authentication and security: Email and password are used to ensure secure access to the platform.
  • User connectivity: Email, name/surname, and parish/team allow you to be identified and connected with other application users.
  • Application improvement and usage statistics: We collect metadata, such as most viewed chants and most used features, anonymously and without direct connection to user identity.

Sharing Information with Third Parties

Laudamus uses third-party services to provide and enhance application features such as secure authentication and data storage, adhering to high security standards. These external services may have limited access to data strictly necessary for application operation, however, all information is handled securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

Laudamus does not share personally identifiable information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or to respond to legal proceedings.
  • To protect the security and integrity of the application, as necessary.

Data Security

We adopt technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, misuse, or improper disclosure. Passwords are stored securely and encrypted, ensuring their protection through Firebase's standards.

User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. To exercise these rights or clarify questions about your information, contact us through the application's support.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the application's data practices. We recommend that you review this page regularly to stay informed.
